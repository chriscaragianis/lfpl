Director - The Louisville Free Public Library—an agency of Louisville Metro Government—is currently seeking an energetic, collaborative, and innovative leader to serve as our next library director. The ideal candidate will work with our talented staff and community residents to continue our tradition of excellence in providing collections and services that meet the diverse needs of more than 756,000 residents.
