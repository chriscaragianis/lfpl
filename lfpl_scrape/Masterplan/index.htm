Louisville Free Public Library Updated Master Plan

This master plan was commissioned by the Louisville Metro Government and is an update of the Master Facilities Plan prepared in December 2002. This report builds on the Master Facilities Plan to further analyze and define the needs for the library through the year 2020. This report also establishes a plan for branch and main library construction and the budgets necessary to bring the LFPL up to minimum State of Kentucky standards.
