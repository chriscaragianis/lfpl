Library Tours

Tours of the Main and Branch libraries are offered year-round. Tours consist of an over-all introduction to library facilities and services. Tours can be tailored as desired. For example, a tour for preschool and primary classes could include a storytime. Also, presentations on requested subjects, such as an introduction to the library's website in one of the library's Computer Learning Centers, can be included. To provide new library cards at the time of the visit, application forms should be returned at least five days prior to the visit. We ask that teachers and adults accompany tours for children.

To schedule Main Library adult tours, contact Main Information Services at 574-1616. To schedule Main Library tours for children, contact the Main Library Children's Department at 574-1620. To schedule tours of branch libraries, contact the library's JustAsk Department at 574-1611. Please schedule tours at least one week in advance.
