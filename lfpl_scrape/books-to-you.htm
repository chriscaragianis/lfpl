Books To You Volunteer Services

Who Can Receive Books through the Books To You Volunteer Service?

Any resident of Jefferson County who is physically unable to come to the Library.

When Are the Materials Delivered?

Once a month, a volunteer will deliver library materials and accept requests for the next delivery.

What Materials Are Available?

Any non-reference item in our collection can be delivered including large print and regular print books, books on CD, Tape, or Playaway, Dvds and music cds. 

Getting Started By Completing the Online Application

Click on a Library location below to complete an ONLINE copy of the Books to You Application for that location.

Additional Questions And How To Get Started?

Please call the branch closest to you they will be happy to talk to you about the service and see if you qualify. Or you can get started by completing the online application for your closest branch and someone will be in touch soon.   Applications can be found at

Who Can Be a Books To You Volunteer?

People who want to share their love of reading, who enjoy making new friends, and who have reliable transportation.

When Will I Deliver the Books To You Materials?

Materials are delivered once a month at a time arranged by the volunteer.

How Can I Become a Volunteer?

Please complete our Volunteer Applications and check the box for Homebound Services. More volunteers are always needed! Training is provided.
