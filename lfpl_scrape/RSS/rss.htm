RSS at LFPL

Learn about New Books, Sound Recordings, and Videos @ LFPL

As new titles are added to the library catalog, lists will be distributed through RSS and Email.  To subscribe, follow these directions.

Subscribe to an RSS Feed of New Titles. (What is an RSS feed?)

  1. Click on the orange XML button next to the topic of interest.
  2. Follow the instructions of your news or feed reader to subscribe to the RSS Feeds. (To view the feed's url, right click on the XML button to copy the link location.)

Subscribe to a Daily Email of New Titles.

  1. Click on the gray Email button next to the topic of interest.
  2. Enter your email address and click on "Subscribe Me!"
