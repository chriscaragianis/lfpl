Wireless Internet Access at the Library

Directions for Changing Internet Browser Settings with Internet Explorer

  1. Click on Tools menu.
  2. Click on Internet Options.

  1. Click on Conections.
  2. Click on LAN Settings.
  3. Enter proxy1.lfpl.org. The port is 80.

Directions for Changing Internet Browser Settings with MAC OS X

  1. Click the apple icon at the left end of the main menu bar.
  2. Click System Preferences
  3. Click Network
  4. Select Automatic for Location
  5. Select Airport next to Show.
  6. Click Proxies.
  7. Check Web Proxy (HTTP).
  8. Enter proxy1.lfpl.org for the proxy name, and 80
