The Art of Summer Reading
A poster art retrospective from the Louisville Free Public Library archives

Since 1929, the Louisville Free Public Libraryâ€™s Summer Reading Program has served hundreds of thousands of children in and around the Louisville community.

The Art of Summer Reading showcases nearly 40 years of Summer Reading poster art and contains memorabelia from the 1950s through the 2000s. The exhibit includes work by nationally award-winning and bestselling children's book illustrators Eric Rohmann, Bryan Collier, Chris Raschka, Michael Emberley, and Nina Crews, as well as local favorites by John Nation, Pip Pullen, and Mary Dennis Kannapell.
