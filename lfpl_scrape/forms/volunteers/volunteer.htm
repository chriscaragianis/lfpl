Volunteering for the Library

The Louisville Free Public Library welcomes the assistance of volunteers to support and extend the services of the library.

The use of volunteers varies at each library location based on the number of volunteers already working and the services provided. (Main and Branch Locations and Hours).

All volunteers must complete the following forms before starting:

  1. Volunteer Application Form (see below)
  2. Worker's Compensation Form (filled out at the branch)
  3. Authorization for a Criminal Records Check (filled out at the branch)

If you have questions about adult volunteering at the Main Library, please contact Carrick Arehart at 574-1712.

If you have questions about volunteering in the Children's Department or Underground teen center at the Main Library, please contact Peter Howard at 574-1815. (download the Youth Volunteer Application form - requires Adobe Acrobat Reader)

If you have questions about volunteering at your neighborhood branch, please contact David Kniss in Branch Services at 574-1635.
