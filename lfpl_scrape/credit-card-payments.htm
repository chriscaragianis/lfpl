Pay Your Fines Online

In an effort to better serve Library Card holders, the Library is now offering Online Credit Card Bill Pay at all LFPL locations.  This is a fast, easy, and secure way for our patrons to pay their Library Account.  Just log on to www.lfpl.org and follow the instructions below.

To Pay Online

  • From any page of our Web site, click “My Account”
  • Access your account by entering your library card number and password
  • Click “Fines and Fees”
  • Select the items you wish to pay and click “Pay Fines Now”
  • Enter your credit card number, security code, card type, and expiration date in the fields provided
  • Click on “Next” at the bottom of the page to complete the transaction
