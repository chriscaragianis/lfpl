Children's Bookmobile

The Louisville Free Public Library operates a children's bookmobile during the school year and summer months. The bookmobile visits schools, community centers, and daycare centers on a monthly basis. If you would like to apply for bookmobile service at your site, please fill out this form.

The bookmobile serves primarily preschoolers, but also sites where accessibility to books is difficult and sites that are not near a library branch. Though our yearly schedule is set, we are able to send the bookmobile or a library staff member to special one-time events or festivals for library card signups, book check outs, or storytimes. To schedule this for your event, please fill out this form.

 

Adult Bookmobile

Books to You is the Library's Adult Outreach service. Though a little different from the traditional bookmobile service, Books To You delivers books and provide library outreach to qualifying group residences. We can also deliver items through the mail to qualifying individuals.  For more information on Books To You and LFPL's Adult Outreach Services, click here.
