Book Discussion Kits

LFPL’s Book Discussion Kits have everything you need for a successful group reading experience.

Each kit consists of 10 books along with author information, reviews and suggested discussion topics, all packed in a convenient canvas tote.  Kits may be reserved and sent to any branch for pickup and can be checked out for 6 weeks.

The nearly 500 titles in the collection cover a wide variety of reading interests.  A limited number of kits are also available in large print.

For more information, please call 574-1611.
