Downloadable Audiobooks

The Louisville Free Public Library's popular downloadable audiobook collection is now available through OneClickdigital. After a quick one-time account set-up process, you can checkout and download audiobooks for free.

If you need further assistance, please email OneClickdigital Support or call toll-free 877-77-AUDIO.

  • OneClickdigital Step-By-Step Instructions

Additional free downloadable audio books can be found online.  Here are a few websites that contain free downloadable audio books and a custom search engine for searching for downloadable audio books.

  • LibriVox - Includes public domain books, poetry, and dramatic works read by volunteers. 
  • Podiobooks.com - Includes serialized audio books available through direct download or RSS feeds.
  • Project Gutenberg - Includes human read and computer generated audio books.

    Search for Free Downloadable Audio Books

     

And don't forget, you can checkout audiobooks on CD at any of our library branches or place a hold to pick up an audiobook at your favorite branch.
