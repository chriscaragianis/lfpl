Class Visits to the Library

Bring your class in for a storytime and tour of the children's department and explore some of the materials and services we have to offer!

  • Please schedule class visits at least three (3) weeks in advance
  • If you would like to get library cards for your class, please contact the library you will be visiting to arrange this so the cards will be ready for checkout on the day of your visit.
  • Most class visits include a storytime, tour of the library, and time for check out.  If you would like to tailor a visit to your specific needs, please contact the Children's staff at the location you will be visiting.

We also offer a variety of programs that are appropriate for groups. Please see our events page.
