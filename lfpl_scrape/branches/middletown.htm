Middletown Library

200 North Juneau Drive Louisville, Kentucky 40243 | (502) 245-7332

Map | History | Upcoming Programs | Volunteer | Meeting Room Request

The Middletown Library, located in far Eastern Jefferson County, serves residents from a municipal building also housing the Louisville Metro Police’s 8th Division, a County Clerk’s office, Driver’s Licensing bureau, Vocational Rehabilitation, and a Department of Health office. (more...)

Directions

 From downtown take either I-71 or I-64:

I-71 - Take Interstate 71 north from Downtown to Exit 9A, the Gene Snyder Freeway (Interstate 265/Kentucky 841).  Go south on the Gene Snyder Freeway 7.5 miles to Exit 27, U.S. 60/Shelbyville Road.  Take a right onto Shelbyville Road.  Go approximately one mile and turn right off Shelbyville Road onto Juneau Drive; directly behind the McDonalds is the East Government Center.  The front of the building also has a sign that reads MIDDLETOWN LIBRARY.  Entering the building, the Library is located straight ahead.

I-64 - Take Interstate 64 east from Downtown to exit 19B, the Gene Snyder Freeway (Interstate 265/Kentucky 841).  Go north on the Gene Snyder Freeway to exit 27 (U.S. 60/Shelbyville Road).  Turn left onto Shelbyville Road.  Go approximately one mile and turn right off Shelbyville Road onto Juneau Drive; directly behind the McDonald's is the East Government Center.  The front of the building also has a sign that reads MIDDLETOWN LIBRARY.   Entering the building, the Library is located straight ahead.

From Hurstbourne Lane - From Hurstbourne Lane, take Shelbyville Road east approximately three miles. McDonald’s is located at the intersection of Shelbyville Road and Juneau Drive; directly behind the McDonald's is the East Government Center. Turn left off Shelbyville Road onto Juneau Drive. The front of the building also has a sign that reads MIDDLETOWN LIBRARY.  Entering the building the Library is located straight ahead.

History

The original Middletown Branch opened in Eastern High School in 1962 and was one of four Louisville Free Public Library Libraries located in Jefferson County Public Schools. This library closed in 1980, reopening on April 29, 1985, in a shared building with offices of the City of Middletown. The Library outgrew this location and in February of 1989 moved to its present location in the East Government Center.
