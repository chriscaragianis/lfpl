What You Need to Get Started

  • An internet connection via PC, Mac, or select mobile devices.
  • A valid library card and your password (Need a card? click here).
  • Free, required software:
    • Adobe Digital Editions - for Adobe PDF and EPUB eBooks
    • OverDrive App - for SmartPhones and tablets
    • OverDrive App for Nook - for the Nook tablet
    • OverDrive Read – read in a web browser on a computer or mobile device
    Software not required for Kindle, but you must have an Amazon account.

How It Works

Step 1: Browse
Browse the Library's eBook Collection – search books by title, subject, author, or format.

Step 2: Borrow
Click “Borrow” to check out the book.
If a title we own is not available, you may place a hold. You’ll receive an email when the title is available.

Step 3: Download and enjoy!
Click “Read” to read the book in a compatible browser.  To download the book, click “download” and select the format compatible for your device. Titles will automatically expire at the end of the lending period.  There are no late fees!
